## XLR Microphones
For those looking to elevate their audio quality further, XLR microphones provide superior sound and versatility but come with a more complex setup. You will need an audio interface to connect them to your computer.

### Key Considerations:
- **Sound Quality:** XLR microphones generally offer better sound quality compared to USB mics.
- **Budget:** USB mics are usually less expensive, making them a good choice for beginners.
- **Ease of Use:** USB mics are plug-and-play, while XLR mics require additional equipment.

## Optimizing Your Audio
To ensure professional-sounding streams:
- Invest in a good microphone to capture clear audio.
- Use pop filters and shock mounts to reduce unwanted noise.
- Experiment with your audio settings to find the best balance.
