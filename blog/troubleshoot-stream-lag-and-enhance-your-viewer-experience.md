## Introduction
Stream lag can be one of the most frustrating issues for any streamer. Whether you're new to the game or a seasoned pro, lag can affect your viewer's experience and ultimately your channel's growth. In this guide, we'll explore common causes of stream lag and provide actionable solutions to optimize your setup.

## Common Causes of Stream Lag
- **Internet Connection**: A slow or unstable connection can lead to buffering and lag. 
- **Hardware Limitations**: Outdated or insufficient hardware can struggle to process streaming tasks efficiently. 
- **Software Settings**: Incorrect settings in your streaming software can contribute to lag.
