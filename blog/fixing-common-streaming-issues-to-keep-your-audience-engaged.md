## Common Streaming Problems
1. **Lag**: Lag can lead to a frustrating experience for both streamers and viewers. Make sure your internet connection is stable and consider using an Ethernet connection instead of Wi-Fi.
2. **Audio Desync**: If your audio does not match your video, check your streaming software settings and adjust the audio delay if necessary.
3. **Dropped Frames**: Dropped frames can occur due to hardware limitations. Ensure your streaming setup meets the requirements for your desired output quality.
